Ingredients required for batter of 5 servings of delicious Red Velvet Cake
3/4 cup and 1 and 1/2 teaspoon all purpose flour
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup and 2 tablespoon sugar
1/2 cup and 2 tablespoon Refined oil
1/4 teaspoon edible food color
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
3/4 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
3/4 egg
1/4 cup and 3 teaspoon buttermilk
3/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

ICING

1/2 cup cream cheese
1 tablespoon finely chopped almonds
1/4 cup butter
1 cup icing sugar
1 tablespoon finely chopped cashews
